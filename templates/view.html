SOM neurons as data prototypes

SOM neurons spread out on the data manifold becoming data prototypes. You can download the weights of each SOM neuron as a csv file. Each row represents a neuron, and each column corresponds to a feature in your original data.

U-matrix

This plot is a visualization of the Self-Organizing Map (SOM) trained on your data. In this plot, each cell represents a neuron in the map. The color of each cell represents its weight distance to its neighbors. Islands in this map are clusters in your dataset.
